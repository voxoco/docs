If you are having trouble with your microphone features, the following information can help you troubleshoot and resolve issues.

## Make sure apps have access to the microphone

<img
  src="/images/general/headset-issue/image 2.png"
  alt="access to the microphone"
/>

If your microphone isn't detected after updating Windows 10, you may need to give your apps permission to use it.

<ul>
  <li>
    To let apps access the microphone, select <strong>Start</strong> ,then
    select <strong>Settings</strong> > <strong>Privacy</strong> >{' '}
    <strong>Microphone</strong> . Select <strong>Change</strong>, then turn on{' '}
    <strong>Allow apps to access your microphone.</strong>
  </li>
  <li>
    After allowing access to the microphone, you can choose which Microsoft
    Store apps can access these features under{' '}
    <strong>
      Choose which Microsoft Store apps can access your microphone
    </strong>
    , and give access to non-Microsoft-Store desktop apps by ensuring that the switch
    beneath <strong>Allow desktop apps to access your microphone</strong> is set
    to <strong>On</strong>.
  </li>
</ul>

## Others can't hear me

Try the following solutions:

<ul>
  <li>If your headset has a Mute button, make sure it isn't active.</li>
  <li>
    Make sure that your microphone or headset is connected correctly to your
    computer.
  </li>
  <li>
    Make sure that your microphone or headset is the system default recording
    device. Here's how to do this in Windows 10:
  </li>
</ul>

<ol>
  <li>
    Select <strong>Start</strong> , then select <strong>Settings</strong> >{' '}
    <strong>System</strong> > <strong>Sound.</strong>
  </li>
  <li>
    In <strong>Input</strong>, ensure your microphone is selected in{' '}
    <strong>Choose your input device.</strong>
  </li>
  <li>
    To test your microphone, speak into it and check{' '}
    <strong>Test your microphone</strong> to make sure Windows is hearing you.
  </li>
</ol>

## Allowing Microphone for applications in Google Chrome

Open Chrome .

<ol>
  <li>
    At the top right, click More <strong>Settings</strong>.
  </li>
  <li>
    Under "Privacy and security," click <strong>Site settings.</strong>
  </li>
  <li>
    Click <strong>Camera</strong> or <strong>Microphone:</strong>
    <ol>
      <li>
        Turn on or off <strong>Ask before accessing.</strong>
      </li>
      <li>Review your blocked and allowed sites.</li>
      <li>
        <strong>To remove an existing exception or permission</strong>: To the
        right of the site, click Delete.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>To allow a site that you already blocked</strong>: Under
        "Blocked," select the site's name and change the camera or microphone
        permission to "Allow."
      </li>
    </ol>
  </li>
</ol>
