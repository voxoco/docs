# How to: Factory reset (Yealink series)

Instructions on how to reset or factory reset your VOXO Yealink phone

If you need to factory reset your VOXO Yealink phone, follow the instructions below:

<ol>
  <li>Hold down the <strong>OK</strong> for several seconds.</li>
  <li>
    A confirmation screen will show asking for permission to factory reset.
    Press the <strong>OK Soft Key</strong> to reset.
  </li>
</ol>

The phone will reboot and reset.
