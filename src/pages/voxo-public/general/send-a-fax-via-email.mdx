# Send a fax via email

First you will start a new email. 

The <strong>To</strong> you will enter [send@voxofax.com](send@voxofax.com).

The <strong>Subject</strong> will be the telephone number you want to send the fax to. Just enter 10 digits (no spaces or dashes)

Simply attach a PDF to the email body and Send.

Only PDF format is supported for sending faxes.
