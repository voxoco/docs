# How to Connect Yealink Phone to WiFi

Step-by-step instructions on connecting your Yealink deskphone to your WiFi network
​

<span className="block">
  <strong>Compatibility</strong>
</span>

Below are the compatible phone models:

<ul>
    <li>
        T42S and T46S could support WF40 (WiFi adapter) with a frequency range of 2.4GHz.
    </li>
    <li>
        T48S could support the WF50 (2.4GHz or 5GHz) and WF40 adapter(2.4GHz).
    </li>
    <li>
        T5xW could support built-in WiFi and does not need a WiFi adapter.
    </li>
    <li>
        T4xU could support the WF50 (2.4GHz or 5GHz) and WF40 adapter(2.4GHz).
    </li>
</ul>
​
<span className="block">
  <strong>Connecting Yealink phones to Wi-Fi</strong>
</span>

To start connecting your Yealink device to WiFi , follow the below steps:

<ol>
  <li>
    Insert WiFi adapter into the USB port on the back of the phone if needed
  </li>
  <li>
    You will be prompted to scan and connect to available Wi-Fi networks.
    Alternatively, you can do this manually by going to <strong>Menu</strong> > <strong>Settings</strong> > <strong>Basic
    Settings</strong> > <strong>Wi-Fi>.</strong>
  </li>
  <li>Select <strong>Available Networks</strong>, and press <strong>OK.</strong></li>
  <li>Select the desired Wi-Fi network and press <strong>Connect.</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the password, and press <strong>OK.</strong></li>
</ol>
