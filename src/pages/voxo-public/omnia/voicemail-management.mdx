# Voicemail Management

Manage your voicemail settings

You can find this in the user settings portion of Omnia. From your avatar dropdown, navigate to settings and the new Voicemail tab.
You can:
<ul>
    <li>
        Modify your voicemail pin / email
    </li>
    <li>
        Manage your Voicemail greetings
    </li>
</ul>
